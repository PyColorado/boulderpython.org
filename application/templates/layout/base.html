ABOUT US

We are a group of python developers, scientists, automators, and enthusiasts that gather once a month in Boulder, Colorado to discuss all things python. We usually meet on the 2nd Tuesday of each month. You can find and engage with us on Meetup, Twitter, and Slack in the (#meetup-python) and (#help-python) topics.
